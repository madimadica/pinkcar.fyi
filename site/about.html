About

I'm Madi and this is my 2025 Honda Civic Type-R (R-47483). This site is an extension of my Instagram to show the extra details along my journey to build my perfect dream car.

My interest in cars was influenced by a variety of things when I was a kid such as the Fast and Furious franchise and Need For Speed Most Wanted (NFSMW). What stuck with me the most from NFSMW was Razor's BMW M3 and it's gigantic GT Wing. Driving around Rockport with that thing was so iconic. It's the biggest reason why I have such a big wing on my car now.

Skipping ahead, in 2021 I saw a Championship White FK8 Type-R in the wild and instantly fell in love with it - especially the wing. I took a picture to ask my brother what it was. I then learned that it was a Type-R, but that I had nowhere near enough money to buy one. I really wanted it to be my next car, but unfortunately 2021 was the last model year for the FK8.

Then towards the end of 2025, my old car I had since highschool was starting to have some minor issues pop up, and my brother was pushing me to buy a new car. Now I had some money to spend on it, and I narrowed my search down to a WRX, Civic/Type-R, or a Miata. I had also realized at this time that I didn't like the FL5 pictures as much as the FK8, but when I saw it in person it really started to grow on me. I was also worried about the WRX blowing up based on anecdotal trends, and heard some negative reviews of daily driving a Miata that I put it on the backburner. As well as that a lightweight RWD was probably not good in the winter. So I eventually focused back on the Type-R, but I really wanted an FK8. Unfortunately it was a big struggle to find any reasonable FK8s, so I decided to buy a new FL5 while I still could, and later try to collect a used FK8. I've never driven manual prior to this car, but it was so much easier to learn in a new Type-R than any other manual.

I knew going into it that I wanted to wrap the car in pink and turn it into my dream car. But there's so many other little things you can do to make it more cute/feminine, so I'm going to use this website to track all the things I do to it, what it costs, and and comments I have on it (good or bad).
